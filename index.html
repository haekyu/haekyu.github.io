About Me

I am interested in drawing out a core insight from intertwined and inundated data these days and utilize the data more creatively. My research currently focuses on machine learning and data mining. You can see my related research works below.

Projects

  • Recommender system on videos of Oksusu application (with SKT, Aug-Present, 2017)
    We recommend videos to users in Oksusu application. Our model uses deep learning to achieve quality recommendation. Our recommendation engine handles massive data on users' behaviors and heterogeneous information on videos.
  • Personalized recommendation on credit card benefits (with Hyundai card, June-Dec, 2016)
    We provide personalized recommendations on credit card benefits to customers. The main algorithm is TCMF (Time Coupled Matrix Factorization). We use various side information of users and items and highly improve the recommendation performance.
