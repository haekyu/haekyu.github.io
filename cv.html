Haekyu Park
I'm a Machine Learning Researcher/Engineer, focusing on ML interpretability and human-centered AI, enhanced through interactive visualization techniques.
I received Ph.D. in Computer Science at Georgia Tech, advised by Dr. Polo Chau. My PhD research combined machine learning with interactive visualizations to make ML models more interpretable, trustworthy, and safe. The core of my research involved:
  • Developing interactive visualization systems that scale to summarize and elucidate concepts learned by large-scale DNNs.
  • Exploring how these concepts form and evolve during training, by visually tracking the inception and evolution of such learned concepts over time.
  • Creating interactive tools to identify and visually explain vulnerabilities in DNNs, enhancing understanding of how models can be manipulated or fail when the models are at risk from potential threats.
My research was supported by JPMorgan AI PhD Fellowship.
After completing my PhD, I worked as a Machine Learning Engineer at Stripe, where I developed AI models tailored for high-stakes environments. At stripe, I deveolped advanced ML models for detecting potential fraud, and assessed the impact of AI on users within sensitive financial contexts.
